A LOSER'S COMPLETE MANUAL TO LIVING LIFE | 06-05-2025

Contrary to popular belief, we were not put on earth with a manual on how to live life, so I have put it upon myself to create one! This manual takes the form of a tree structure, with two distinct types of nodes:

  1. A chapter node, that talks about a specific part of life
  2. A challenge node, that gives you something specific to do

The tree follows three rules:

  1. Nodes closer to the root are always more important
  2. All parents of a challenge node must be completed
  3. Each child is just as important as any of their siblings

The manual has five initial chapters:

VIRTUE How good you are to others?

BODY Do you take care of your body?

MIND What is going on in your mind?

FOOD What are you made of?

SOUL What are you even doing here?
