A LOSER'S COMPLETE MANUAL TO LIVING LIFE | 06-05-2025

[Guide Node] Contrary to popular belief, we were not put on earth with a manual on how to live life, so I have put it upon myself to create one! This manual takes the form of a tree structure, with two distinct types of nodes:

  1. A guide node, that talks about a specific part of life (like this one)
  2. A challenge node, that gives you something specific to do

The tree follows three rules:

  1. Nodes closer to the root are always more important than ones lower down
  2. All parents of a challenge node must be completed
  3. Each child is just as important as any of their siblings

The manual starts with the following five guide nodes:

  • Virtue How good you are to others?
  • Body Do you take care of your body?
  • Mind What is going on in your mind?
  • Food What are you made of?
  • Soul What are you even doing here?
