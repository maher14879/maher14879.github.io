What is the secret number?

  • Each even digit is equal to the number of occurrences of itself
  • The middle digit is the number of digits greater than 5
  • The number is palindromic
  • The sum of prime digits is palindromic and has more than 1 digit
  • The length of the number is a square number
  • All primes digits are touching at least one other prime
The answer is: 666272666
